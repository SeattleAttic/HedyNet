Donations: Thank You!

Thank you for supporting the Seattle Attic Community Workshop. We are a fiscally sponsored project of the School Factory, Inc. a 501(c)3 non-profit corporation. They will provide you with a receipt for your donation.

Many companies will match donations to 501c3 charities. Microsoft employees, for example, can go to MS Give (an intranet link) and search for School Factory to have donations matched. Please indicate in the comments on your donation that it is intended for the Seattle Attic Community Workshop so that School Factory can route it appropriately.
