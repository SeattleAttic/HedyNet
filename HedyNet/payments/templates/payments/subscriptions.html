Member Subscriptions

Hello, charming Attic member! This is where you can set up a recurring payment for your Workshop membership. Thank you for doing your part to support our space! We currently have three levels of membership.

Membership Levels

Full

Members have access to the space and can attend members only meetings.

Starving Hacker

If the regular membership rate would cause hardship, members may elect to pay this rate instead.

Friends of the Attic

A level for people who want to show their support and help keep the space running, but aren't interested in membership.

PayPal

PayPal has bigger processing fees than Dwolla, but you can set up a recurring subscription so you don't have to remember to manually pay each month. PayPal can also accept credit cards, unlike Dwolla.
